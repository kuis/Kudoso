Content Monitoring
& Filtering

Kudoso provides the most robust solution for content filtering and monitoring in the industry. In a technology driven world, your child will be exposed to unfiltered, unwanted content every single day that is not in line with your family values. At Kudoso, we believe you as the parent need to be aware of this exposure and in control of it to ensure the innocence of your children’s minds as well as their overall wellbeing. Through a unique combination of hardware and software, Kudoso protects your kids both inside your home and outside of it through our Whole Home Technology Solution.

Protect Features

Our mission is to help parents control the influence of technology on their children through mutual cooperation and the positive reinforcement of family values and life skills.

  • Kudoso Gateway is a hardware filtering device protecting all the electronics in your home, even guest devices, providing parents with a true “Whole Home Solution” to tech management.

  • Real-time Filtering content changes every second of the day, and predators are actively targeting your children through sites they think your child will innocently click on. Our cutting edge system is one step ahead of them…filtering each and every page in real time each and every time.

  • Simple & Easy The Kudoso hardware is simple to install (think less than 10 minutes) and the software was created with the busy parent in mind. So with a couple simple clicks, your home and kids are protected within minutes.

  • Mobile Friendly software protects your children both in the home and outside of it. No matter where they are, Kudoso helps you keep them safe and supports iOS & Android tablets as well as smart phones.

  • Completely Customizable because no two children are alike, we give you the power to choose with the click of a button what each child’s content filter looks like as well as a household default for guests in your home connected to your Wi-Fi. Don’t have time to customize? No problem, we have predefined filters available to help you get started now and customize later.

  • Powerful Reports give you the tools you need to be a strategic, intentional parent. Web traffic is reported in 32 categories to make it easy to understand and adjust to meet the needs of your family.

  • Parental Overrides allow your child to request access to blocked content in real-time, sending you a notification to allow it or keep it blocked with ease. No need to call you at work if blocked content is hindering their school work. They request it, and you approve it …all over your Kudoso applications instantly.

  • Just Plug In a device designed to set itself up! It is really that easy! We also offer customer service based right here in the US of A!

Hardware + Software = Best Protection

Kudoso is passionate about protecting your kids. So we didn't go the "easy route" and buy old, outdated lists of websites and try to use that as a filter. Instead we built an amazing hybrid solution of hardware and software that filters and protects in real time.

Kudoso Gateway: Whole-home Protection

Kudoso makes it easy protect your entire house by monitoring and filtering traffic at the network level. The Kudoso Gateway is a powerful wireless router that is placed at the core of your network where all traffic bound for the internet is aggregated. From there, we scan and filter the content in real-time using both the Kudoso Gateway and our powerful cloud filtering platform.
